Our Facilities

Welcome to our school's facilities page. We are proud to offer a range of top-notch facilities to support our students' academic and extracurricular activities.

Library

Our library is stocked with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support students' learning and research needs. It provides a quiet and conducive environment for study.

Science Laboratories

We have state-of-the-art science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. These labs are equipped with modern equipment and materials to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation.

Sports Complex

Our sports complex includes a gymnasium, swimming pool, and various courts for basketball, tennis, and volleyball. We encourage students to participate in sports to promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Computer Labs

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest technology and high-speed internet access. Students can use these facilities for coding, research, and other computer-based activities.

Art and Music Rooms

We have dedicated rooms for art and music where students can explore their creativity. These rooms are equipped with various instruments and art supplies to support students' artistic endeavors.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers a variety of healthy and delicious meals and snacks. It is a great place for students to relax and socialize during their breaks.
